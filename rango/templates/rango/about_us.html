About LiftLogic

HomePage

LiftLogic is a smart workout app designed with the purpose to assist you in progressing your lifting journey with confidence. LiftLogic creates a personalized strength program tailored to your current ability and personal best rep after entering your one rep max.

Based on your performance, your program is adapted in each week to maximize gain and recovery. when using LiftLogic, logging your previous lifts, tracking your progress, and providing feedback on the workout difficulty is possible allowing LiftLogic to provide recommendations such as increasing your weight, manage recovery, or even try different strength programs.

Founded in 2026 by a team of five University of Glasgow students, LiftLogic was created with the aim to make structured strength training easily and readily accessable to all. The program is user friendly enabling beginners just starting out in the gym to experienced veterans lifters looking to go above and beyond to use the app with ease.
