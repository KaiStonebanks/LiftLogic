About LiftLogic

LiftLogic is a smart workout app designed with the purpose to assist you in progressing your lifting journey with confidence. LiftLogic creates a personalized strength program tailored to your current ability and personal best rep.

Based on your performance, your program is adapted in each week to maximize gain and recovery. When using LiftLogic, logging your previous lifts and tracking your progress is possible.

Founded in 2026 by a team of University of Glasgow students, LiftLogic was created to make structured strength training accessible to all.
